    • Phase 01

    Discovery & Strategy

    Problem Statement

    Bridging the gap between ambitious business requirements and actual user behavior. Many products fail not due to lack of features, but due to lack of structural clarity and alignment with real-world problems.

    Business Goals

    Establishing clear objectives: Revenue targets, user retention metrics, and market positioning strategy. Aligning stakeholders on a unified vision.

    User Goals

    Identifying core pain points. What is the "Job to be Done"? Ensuring the product serves a functional purpose that simplifies the user's life.

    Success Metrics (KPIs)

    Defining what success looks like: Conversion rates, Task Success Rate (TSR), and Time on Task (ToT). These metrics drive every subsequent design decision.

    Phase 02

    Research & Synthesis

    Competitive Systems Analysis

    Researching market leaders and adjacent industries to deconstruct their information architecture. I look for established mental models that users already understand to reduce the learning curve.

    [ Competitive Landscape & Feature Mapping Visual ]

    User Journey Mapping

    Visualizing the end-to-step-by-step experience. This phase identifies potential friction points and "Aha!" moments where the user realizes the product's value.

    Phase 03

    Information Architecture & Logic

    Structural Foundation

    Building the skeleton of the system. I focus on hierarchical clarity, ensuring that information is organized logically and is easily discoverable.

    Ideal User Flows

    Designing the "Happy Path"—the most efficient route for a user to achieve their primary goal with zero friction.

    Edge Cases & Error States

    Designing for when things go wrong. How does the system handle empty states, lost connections, or invalid inputs? Logic must be bulletproof.

    [ Complex User Flow & Logic Diagram Visual ]

    Wireframing & Cognitive Load

    Low-fidelity structures that prioritize function over form. Here, I test the layout's ability to communicate clearly without the distraction of visual styling.

    Phase 04

    Design System & UI Architecture

    Design Tokens

    Codifying colors, typography, spacing, and shadows into a system of variables. This ensures absolute consistency and allows for rapid global updates.

    Scalable Component Library

    Building atomic components (buttons, inputs, cards) that can be combined into complex organisms. Modular design for a modular world.

    [ Component Library & Grid System Visual ]

    Accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA)

    Design is for everyone. I ensure high contrast ratios, clear focus states, and keyboard navigability to meet international accessibility standards.

    Phase 05

    Handoff & Documentation

    Technical Specs & States

    Clean handoff is crucial. I provide developers with detailed specifications, including responsive behavior, animation timing, and all interactive states (hover, active, disabled).

    [ Documentation & Handoff Spec Visual ]

    By providing a single source of truth, we reduce development time and ensure the final product matches the architectural vision perfectly.
